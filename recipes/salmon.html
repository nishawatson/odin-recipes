Crispy Baked Salmon

This simple yet delicious baked salmon will be your go to weeknight dinner. Ready in about 25 minutes, this tangy, sweet, savory, crispy recipe will be sure to hit the spot for everyone in your family.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup parsley leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 pounds skin-on salmon fillet, center-cut
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  3. Mix brown sugar, paprika, garlic, cayenne pepper, 1 tablespoon of salt, and a generous amount of a pepper in a bowl.
  4. In another bowl, mix the panko with the parsley and butter. Add 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt and afew grinds of black pepper.
  5. Place the salmon skin-side down on the baking sheet and spread the surface with Dijon.
  6. Bake until the breadcrumbs are golden brown. 15-18 minutes.
  7. Cut into four equal portions and serve.
