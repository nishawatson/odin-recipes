Creamy Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Classic mashed potatoes. Girl dinner made from scratch.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 pounds russet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons Kosher salt
  • 2 cups half-and-half
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 6 ounces grated Parmesan

Directions

  1. Peel and dice potatoes. Make sure they are all relatively the same size.
  2. Place potatoes in a large saucepan. Add salt and cover with water.
  3. Bring pot to a boil over medium-high heat and then reduce to maintain a rolling boil. Cook unitl potatoesfall apart when poked with a fork.
  4. Heat the half-and-half and the garlic in a medium saucepan over medium heat until simmering. Remove from heat and seat aside.
  5. Remove potatoes from heat and drain.
    6. \
  6. Mash and add the garlic-cream mixture and Parmensan; stir to combine.
  7. Let stand 5 minutes until mixture thickens and then serve.
