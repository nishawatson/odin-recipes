Bacon Wrapped Asparagus

A perfect addition to your weekend barbecue, this recipe is a quick and tasty way to put some extra greens on your plate!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds asparagus spears, trimmed 4-5 inches long tips
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for drizzling
  • A few grinds black pepper
  • 4 slices center cut bacon or pancetta
  • Chopped chives or scallions, optional garnish

Directions

  1. If using an oven, preheta to 400 degrees.
  2. Lightly coat asparagus spears in extra-virgin olive oil and season with black pepper.
  3. Divide spears evenly into four groups.
  4. Wrap each group of spears in a slice of bacon/pancetta. Repeat with remaining groups of asparagus.
  5. To grill, place bundles on hot grill and cover. Cook 10-12 minutes or until bacon is crisp and asparagus bundles are tender.
  6. For oven preperation, place bundles on slotted broiler pan. Bake 12 minutes.
